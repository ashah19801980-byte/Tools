Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image compressor

How does the image compression work?

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to analyze and optimize your images. For JPG files, it adjusts the compression level while maintaining visual quality. For PNGs, it uses lossless compression techniques. The processing happens entirely in your browser for maximum privacy and speed.

Is there a limit to file size?

We currently support images up to 10MB in size. This limit ensures good performance across all devices. For larger images, we recommend resizing them before compression or using specialized desktop software.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens locally in your web browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security. Once you close the page, all image data is permanently deleted.

What image formats are supported?

We support the most common web image formats: JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. You can choose to keep the original format or convert to any of these formats during compression.

Is this service really free?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden costs. We don't add watermarks or limit the number of images you can process. The service is supported by non-intrusive advertisements.
